Frequently Asked Questions

How can i apply to job that i want

Firstly, you have to look at the job in jobs list. After that, you have to go to specific job, read description and requirement for that job. If you feel that it is job suitable for you, remember the reference number and press the apply button or go to apply page to fill in the candidate information. Remember to submit the form and HR will process your information, response if you meet the requirement.

How can i know my submit form is valid

After you submit the form, you will receive confirmation within 2 hours from the system. You will have ticket number for your submission.

How long from the day i submitted to the day i receive an interview

It has to base on the HR to process your application, if you meet the requirement, you will receive email to go for an interview
