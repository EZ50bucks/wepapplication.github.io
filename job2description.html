Junior network Engineering - Reference number GHIKL

Role

Joining to NOC team, you have responsible to maintain whole system. Providing comprehensive support service, security platform, fault investigation and analysis, specialising in Core network. Ensuring the availability of client/server applications, developing processes and procedures to manage network and infrastructure. Besides that, you have to go outside to deploy project to our partners including set up leased line and wifi system. The primary responsibilities for this position will be networking, however there will be some server and other system management required. This role required support 24/7 business and sometimes it will be required to provide after-hours support.

Requirement

To be selected for interview and become ideal candidate, you must meet some requirements:

  1. Essential
    • 1 year practical hands-on experience.
    • Qualifications in IT especially in network and telecommunication or related discipline.
    • Experience with IP Routing, LAN, Wireless Networks and WAN services.
    • Familiar with commercial network monitoring/analysis tools (Solarwinds, NetFlow, Nagios, Wireshark).
  2. Preferable
    • Having knowledge and experience on troubleshooting and resolving network operation.
    • Experience with Cisco router/Switch technologies: Cisco router, Cisco Switch 2k/3k/4k, Nexus Switchese and Juniper devices.
    • Team player able to work effectively and shares knowledge and skills
    • Having some of these certifications is an advantage: CCNP R&S or CCIE, MCSA/MCSE, CCNP Sercurity.
